Sailing Supplies

Description

When you go out to sea with your two friends on your trusty raft Highwind, you will surely want these essential meals with you for the journey.

Ingredients

  • 3 mushrooms
  • 1 drinking water
  • 3 fish
  • 2 coconuts
  • 1 seagull egg

Steps

  1. Look in dark places for mushrooms.
  2. Stand under the waterfall for drinking water.
  3. Swim around the shore area to catch fish with your bare hands.
  4. Smack a tree with your wooden sword until coconuts fall on your head.
  5. Steal the seagull egg from the top of a tree near the bridge.
  6. Bring all supplies to Kairi and wait to get jumped by shadows and shot into space.
